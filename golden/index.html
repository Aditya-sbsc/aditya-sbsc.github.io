A Legacy of Flavor & Tradition

Born in the heart of Tokyo in 1999, Golden Bowl was founded by Master Chef Hiroshi Tanaka with a singular vision: to elevate the humble bowl of ramen to an experience worthy of fine dining.

Every ingredient is hand-selected from artisan producers. Our broth simmers for 18 hours. Our noodles are pulled fresh daily. It is not just food ‚Äî it is a legacy passed through generations.
